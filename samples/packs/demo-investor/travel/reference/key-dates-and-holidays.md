# Key dates, holidays & long weekends

> The proactive-surfacing layer: a window scan cross-checks the dates here so the console can surface
> trip-worthy long weekends a reactive live scan would miss. (Sample almanac — US federal holidays,
> which are national/public; swap in your own dates + a `sports-schedules.md` for teams you follow.)
