# narratives/

The longform corpus — your stories, the *why* behind your decisions, the emotional texture. **This is
the product**: it's what lets the harness sound like *you* instead of generic AI (the voice is the moat).
Best built by your agent from normal conversation over time (the `corpus-operator` skill), captured raw
in `captures/` then distilled into narrative files here. Start anywhere; it compounds.
